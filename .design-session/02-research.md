## Neo-Pop Design Characteristics

### Colors
- **Bold, saturated** (not pastels, not muted)
- **Color blocking** - sections in different bright colors
- **Palette ideas for VetHub:**
  - Coral/peachy (#FF6B6B, #FFB4A2)
  - Lavender/purple (#B8B5FF, #957FEF)
  - Lime/mint (#A8E6CF, #DCEDC1)
  - Sunny yellow (#FFE66D, #F7DC6F)
  - Soft cream background (#FFF8F0, #FFFBF5)

### Shapes
- Organic blobs instead of hard rectangles
- Rounded corners (very rounded, 16-24px)
- Wavy section dividers
- Playful geometric accents

### Typography
- Bold, fun fonts
- Mix of weights for hierarchy
- Rounded typefaces feel friendlier

### Elements
- Playful illustrations/icons
- Soft drop shadows
- Micro-animations
- Fun hover states

## Application to VetHub Dashboard

### Task Cards
- Each task type could have a different accent color
- Blob-shaped cards instead of rectangles
- Patient chips with fun colors
- Playful checkmark animations when completed

### Patient List
- Colorful status badges
- Pet species icons (fun illustrated style)
- Soft card backgrounds
- Hover states with color shifts

### Header
- Could have a wavy bottom edge
- Bright accent color
- Fun logo treatment

### Overall
- Cream/warm white background (not stark white)
- Sections divided by color or wavy shapes
- Feels like the Uncle Stinky energy but for a vet clinic app
