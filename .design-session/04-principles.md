## Visual Style

### Neo-Pop Characteristics
- Bold color blocking
- Organic blob shapes
- Very rounded corners (16-24px)
- Playful illustrations/icons
- Fun typography
- Wavy section dividers
- Soft drop shadows

### Typography
- Bold, friendly fonts
- Mix of weights for hierarchy
- Rounded typefaces

### Spacing
- Generous padding (16-24px)
- Breathing room between elements
- Not cramped

### Interactions
- Playful hover states
- Smooth transitions
- Fun micro-animations (checkmarks, etc.)
